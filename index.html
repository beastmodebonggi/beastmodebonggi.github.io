Be confident on the road with other cyclists

Ride with other Cyclists

The Flock App allows cyclists with different experience level to ride together on a specified route. This prevents road bullying from motorized vehicles like cars and motorcycles, and ensures the safety of each rider.

Features

Route Pooling

The Flock App consolidates route requests from different users. Similar routes are merged and form a route option where users can choose from.

Choose your Pace

Route options show the average pace of pooled riders. Choose how fast you want to go, according to your experience.

Flock Leader

The Flock App automatically chooses the most experienced rider to become the flock leader. Flock leaders are in charge of controlling the pace of the flock, and decides when to take a hydration break.

Road Safety

The Flock App values the safety not only of our cyclists, but also the safety of everyone using the road.
